/01problem → solution

Does any of this feel familiar?

Important parts of your customer journey still depend on someone remembering the next step. These are the gaps I fix inside GoHighLevel.

01

A lead fills out your form — and then waits.

No immediate reply or assigned owner. By the time someone notices, the lead may have contacted a competitor.

The solution

Instant lead response and routing

Reply instantly, create the opportunity, assign an owner, and start the next follow-up.

02

Your pipeline no longer reflects reality.

Deals sit in the wrong stages, follow-up gets missed, and no one knows what needs attention.

The solution

Automated pipeline management

Update stages from forms, bookings, replies, and completed actions so the CRM stays accurate.

03

Your funnel, calendar, and CRM feel disconnected.

Bookings happen, but contact data, pipeline stages, and follow-up do not stay connected.

The solution

A connected booking journey

Connect the page, form, calendar, pipeline, confirmations, and reminders into one journey.

04

Your texts aren’t reliably reaching leads.

Incomplete registration or sender configuration can cause messages to fail or reach fewer leads.

The solution

A2P and messaging setup

Complete A2P registration, configure business details, and test messaging before launch.

/04my build framework

A four-step build framework — from audit to handoff.

Every project follows the same sequence so you can review the logic, scope, and customer journey before anything goes live.

01

Discover & audit

We review your current GoHighLevel setup, customer journey, manual work, and communication gaps. You receive a confirmed scope showing what should be fixed, built, or automated first.

02

Map & approve

I map the pipeline stages, triggers, actions, messages, decision branches, and exceptions. You review the proposed system before the build begins.

03

Build & validate

I build the approved system inside your account and test forms, calendars, messages, pipeline updates, notifications, and workflow branches before launch.

04

Launch & handoff

Once approved, I publish the system and walk you through what was built. Documentation and post-launch support are included according to the agreed scope.
