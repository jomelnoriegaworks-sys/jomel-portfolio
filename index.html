Sample build

Smile Society — Dental Clinic Booking Funnel

A complete GHL funnel built around one job: turn a visitor into a booked consultation in under a minute.

Watch the build — 7 min walkthrough
The brief

Local dental clinics lose patients the same way most service businesses do — a pretty website that describes services but never actually asks anyone to book. This funnel fixes that.

What I built
  • A benefit-led hero with trust signals up top and one clear call to action.
  • A problem section that names the real reasons people avoid the dentist, so visitors feel understood before they're sold to.
  • A clean services grid covering everything from checkups to emergencies.
  • Social proof through patient reviews, then a repeated booking CTA so the action is always one tap away.
  • A native GHL booking calendar — every appointment flows straight into the CRM and triggers follow-up automatically.
The result

This is a spec build made to demonstrate the approach. On a live clinic, this structure is designed to convert cold traffic into booked consultations and cut no-shows through automated reminders.

Sample project — built to show the method, not a paid client result.
